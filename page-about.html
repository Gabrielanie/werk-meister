WERKMEISTER FACILITY MANAGERS LIMITED.

A firm of Facility Managers, has vast experience in Facility Management. We are professional Facilities Managers who are trained to ensure inter-alia that the incidence of obsolescence is put in check while at the same time maximizing benefits from facilities. We relieve clients of the responsibilities of having to keep facilities fitting for use at all times.
