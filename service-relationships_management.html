overview

Facilities Management doesn’t only streamline itself to the physical building but it also manages the people in the work place. We provide a 24/7 property Help Desk which provides you with a one-stop communication point to respond to all property related issues. The Help Desk tracks the progress of all queries or service fault, which enables us to measure how well we are responding and closely monitor the performance of the Facilities Management service from your perspective.

In addition to the above we offer the following;

  1. Drawing up of Rules and Regulation that will guide activities within the facility
  2. Maintaining good working relationships with a wide variety of customers, and also maintain the trust and support of these same customers
  3. Necessary skills to build bonds, inspire, influence and develop others.
  4. Management of conflict
